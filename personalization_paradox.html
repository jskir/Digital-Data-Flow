Reflections on "The Personalization Paradox"

Applying a structured content marketing framework to Digital Data Flow in clinical trials

Background

After attending the CDISC Digital Data Flow Solutions Showcase webinar in March 2025, I was inspired by the structured content ideas presented by Regina Lynn Preciado of Content Rules Inc.

To learn more, I got her book "The Personalization Paradox," which is about structured content in a marketing context. As I'm reading, I'm thinking about how the concepts could apply to structured content in clinical trial documents.
